# HIMKOK: Redefining Norwegian Craft Spirits &amp; Cocktail Culture

## Pitch Deck | March 2025

## Our Story

Founded in 2015 as Norway&#39;s first bar with an on\-site distillery, Himkok has become a global benchmark for innovative spirits and cocktail experiences\.

- **Pioneering concept**: Part bar, part distillery, part innovation lab
- **Consistent global recognition**:
    - Ranked \#10 in World&#39;s 50 Best Bars \(2023\) \- Nikka Whisky Highest Climber award
    - Ranked \#1 in Top 500 Best Bars \(2024\)
    - Ranked \#43 in 2022
    - Ranked \#17 in 2020
    - First\-ever Ketel One Sustainable Bar Award winner \(2018\)
- **Norwegian heritage**: &quot;Himkok&quot; means &quot;moonshine&quot; in Norwegian, embodying our connection to local tradition

Led by founder Erk Potur, Himkok has evolved from a single bar into a multi\-faceted spirits and hospitality business with growing national and international presence, proudly showcasing Nordic ingredients through innovative cocktails and sustainable premium spirits\.

## Market Opportunity

The global craft spirits market is experiencing robust growth, projected to reach $80\.43 billion by 2025, growing at a CAGR of 33\.9% from 2020\-2025\. Nordic spirits particularly are gaining international popularity, with exports increasing by over 30% in the last five years\.

**Key growth drivers**:

- Rising consumer preference for premium, authentic spirits with unique stories
- Growing demand for sustainable, locally\-sourced products
- Exploding interest in ready\-to\-drink \(RTD\) premium cocktails
- Nordic cocktail culture gaining global recognition

**Core demographics**:

- Primary customer base: ages 30\-49 \(over 50% of sales\)
- Strong performance in modern convenience, kiosk, and cross\-border retail channels
- Increasing repurchase rates \(Oslo Mule: from 8\.7% to 10\.8%, Oslo Paloma: 18\.6%\)

Himkok is uniquely positioned at the intersection of these trends, with established credentials and infrastructure to capitalize on this growing market\.

## Business Model

Himkok operates a diversified business with multiple robust revenue streams:

### 1\. Bar Operations

- Premium cocktail venue with 450\-guest capacity
- Three distinct bar areas targeting different customer segments
- Revenue: NOK 27M \(2019\)

### 2\. In\-House Distillery

- 10,000 liters annual production capacity
- Produces 80% of spirits used in our cocktails
- Creates unique, premium Norwegian spirits

### 3\. Ready\-to\-Drink \(RTD\) Cocktails

- Strategic partnership with Aass Brewery \(Norway&#39;s oldest brewery\) until Jan 1, 2026
- Distribution in ~470 outlets nationwide
- Available on Norwegian Air flights
- Product formats:
    - 4\.7% ABV canned cocktails \(333ml\) for off\-trade&#x2F;on\-trade
    - 8\-10% ABV cocktails in 20L KeyKegs for on\-trade venues
- Production in batches of 5,000\-30,000 liters
- Over 300,000 liters produced and 225,000 liters sold in the past 24 months
- Award\-winning flagship products:
    - Oslo Mule \(51\.5% of sales\)
    - Oslo Paloma \(18% of sales\)
    - Snake Bite \(7\.4% of sales\)
- Premium, shelf\-stable formulations with proprietary compound system

### 4\. Consulting &amp; Knowledge\-Sharing

- Menu development and bar design services
- Staff training programs
- Operational guidance for hospitality businesses
