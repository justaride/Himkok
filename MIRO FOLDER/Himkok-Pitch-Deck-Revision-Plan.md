# Himkok Pitch Deck Revision Plan

## Executive Summary

Based on the detailed meeting notes, the current pitch deck requires significant revision to properly convey Himkok&#39;s full business ecosystem and growth potential\. The deck needs to be repositioned from presenting just a cocktail bar to showcasing an integrated, multi\-faceted business with several revenue streams and expansion opportunities\.

## Current Strengths to Maintain

- Strong opening with \#11 World&#39;s 50 Best Bars ranking
- Clear presentation of awards and recognition
- Attractive visual design elements
- Emphasis on Norwegian identity and heritage

## Major Gaps to Address

1. **Connect Spirit** is severely underpresented despite being a central strategic business
2. The complete **business structure** and relationships between entities is unclear
3. **Financial data** needs better breakdown by business unit
4. The **whisky project** requires more detailed explanation
5. The **investment ask** needs clear specification
6. **B2B consulting portal** should be better explained

## Revision Process

1. **Data Collection Phase** \(1\-2 weeks\)
    - Gather 2023 financial statements for all entities
    - Request breakdowns of key metrics by business unit
    - Collect specific details on Connect Spirit performance
    - Document current status of whisky project
    - Specify exact investment requirements
2. **Content Development Phase** \(1\-2 weeks\)
    - Restructure story flow to showcase full business ecosystem
    - Develop new sections for underpresented business units
    - Create better visualizations of company structure
    - Enhance financial presentations with actual data
    - Craft clearer growth narrative connecting all businesses
3. **Design Phase** \(1 week\)
    - Ensure visual consistency across all slides
    - Improve diagrams showing business interconnections
    - Add relevant photography of facilities, products, team
    - Create more compelling data visualizations
4. **Review &amp; Finalization Phase** \(1 week\)
    - Internal review with key stakeholders
    - Create versions tailored to different investor types
    - Prepare supplementary materials for detailed questions
