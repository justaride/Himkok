### Business Model

Himkok operates as a premium cocktail bar in Oslo, Norway, under Nye Kråka Bryggeri &amp; Destilleri AS, founded by Erk Potur\. It features an in\-house distillery producing aquavit, gin, and vodka, emphasizing sustainability and local ingredients\. Beyond the bar, Himkok has diversified into ready\-to\-drink \(RTD\) cocktails and consultancy services, enhancing revenue streams\.

### Growth Strategy

Himkok&#39;s growth strategy focuses on expanding its RTD line, with products like Oslo Mule and Oslo Paloma seeing significant production \(over 100,000 liters in less than a year since August 2022\)\. They aim for international market entry, leveraging their brand&#39;s global ranking \(\#10 in 2023 World&#39;s 50 Best Bars\) and sustainability credentials to tap into growing demand for craft spirits\.

### Market Positioning

Himkok is positioned as a leader in Oslo&#39;s high\-end bar segment, competing with bars like Torggata Botaniske and Fuglen\. Their pricing \(200\-230 NOK per cocktail\) is competitive with other World&#39;s 50 Best Bars, such as The American Bar in London \(337\.5\-405 NOK\)\. Their unique in\-house distillery and focus on Nordic flavors set them apart, appealing to consumers seeking premium, sustainable experiences\.
